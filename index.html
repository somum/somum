About Me

Building AI that ships to production

I'm a Senior AI Engineer based in Tokyo, Japan, specializing in the full stack of modern AI — from model research and fine-tuning to scalable inference systems and cloud-native deployment. My work bridges deep learning research and production engineering, ensuring AI solutions are not just accurate but fast, reliable, and maintainable at scale.
